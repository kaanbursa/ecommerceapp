About School

The National Law University and Judicial Academy, Assam (NLUJAA), situated in the heart of Brahmaputra valley, is a premier institution for imparting multi-disciplinary legal education. The University, in keeping with its motto; In pursuit of excellence, has strived to produce world class legal scholars and practitioners since its very inception. NLUJAA boasts of an extremely erudite faculty to nurture and guide its students through its graduate, post-graduate, and doctoral programs.
